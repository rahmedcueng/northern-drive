Used Car Questions

Frequently asked questions

Helpful answers for GTA buyers looking for used cars, pre-owned sedans, compact SUVs, hatchbacks, and commuter-friendly vehicles through Northern Drive.

Does Northern Drive sell used cars in the Greater Toronto Area?

Yes. Northern Drive serves used vehicle buyers across the GTA, including Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Scarborough, North York, Richmond Hill, Pickering, Ajax, and nearby Ontario communities by appointment.

How do I book a used vehicle viewing?

Call or email Northern Drive with the vehicle you are interested in, your preferred viewing time, and any questions about availability, mileage, price, features, or trade-in options.

What types of vehicles does Northern Drive focus on?

Northern Drive focuses on practical non-luxury used vehicles such as sedans, compact SUVs, hatchbacks, and commuter-friendly vehicles suited for everyday driving in Ontario.

Can I ask about trade-in options?

Yes. You can mention your trade-in vehicle during your inquiry, including the year, make, model, mileage, and overall condition, so the next steps can be discussed.
